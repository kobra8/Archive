The Youth Palace in Tarnow for almost 50 years has worked for youth and children. Our form of activities are adressed to participants aged 5 - 21 years. children. The Youth Palace is an institution subordinate to Tarnow local authority and working within the national education system. Our institution is the greatest institution for youth and children in region.

Our sections:

  • Art: painting, scupulture, music, dance, photography,
  • Sport: handball, judo, basketball, fencing,
  • Modelling: radio controlling car models, models etc.
  • Entertaiments: fetivals, contests, happeninigs.

At the Youth Palace also works:

  • Club amateur cinematographers "Szwenk"
  • Art Gallery "palace"
